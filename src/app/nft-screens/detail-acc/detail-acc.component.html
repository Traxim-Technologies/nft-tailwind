Sugar Rush

DESCRIPTION

Sugar rush is the final design from my dream vs. reality collection. I am a heavy candy addict, which will eventually cause me to get ill when I get older.

We all have sugar that we are addicted to. For some, it’s drugs, money, sex, fame, and more. This final design shows what it is like to have a “sugar rush.”
